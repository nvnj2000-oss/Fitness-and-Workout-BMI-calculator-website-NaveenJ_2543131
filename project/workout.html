Workout Plans

Pick one routine and repeat it 3–4 times per week. Rest when you need to.

  • Beginner Full Body
    Squats 3×10 · Pushups 3×8 · Plank 3×30s
    ~25 mins
  • Cardio Blast (No Equipment)
    Jumping Jacks 3×30s · High Knees 3×30s · Burpees 3×10
    ~20 mins
  • Core focus
    Crunches 3×15 · Leg raises 3×12 · Side plank 3×20s/side
    ~15 mins
